# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to creating a welcoming and inclusive environment for all contributors to this research project, regardless of background, identity, or experience level.

## Expected Behavior

All participants are expected to:
- Be respectful in all communications and interactions
- Be inclusive and welcoming to colleagues from diverse backgrounds
- Be professional in addressing disagreements or feedback
- Be collaborative and supportive of others' work
- Give credit appropriately to others' contributions

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not acceptable:
- Harassment, discrimination, or intimidation based on any characteristic
- Offensive comments or personal attacks
- Trolling, spamming, or deliberate disruption
- Sharing others' private information without consent
- Any form of abuse or threatening language

## Reporting Issues

If you witness or experience behavior that violates this Code of Conduct:
1. Contact the maintainers directly via email or private message
2. Provide specific details about the incident
3. All reports will be taken seriously and handled confidentially

## Consequences

Violations may result in:
- Warning or request to modify behavior
- Temporary or permanent removal from the project
- Escalation to institutional oversight bodies if necessary
